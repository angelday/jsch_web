Personal tidbits about me

I’m originally from Hungary, happily married, a dad of three, and now living in southern Sweden.

In my free time, I play Nintendo games, enjoy retro platformers, stay active, and get happily lost in pixel art.

My first name, József, is the Hungarian version of Joseph. In Hungarian, it’s often shortened to Józsi. When pronounced in English, it can sound a bit like Yoshi. The nickname stuck, and that’s what most people call me. (My GitHub/scene handle is angelday. Don’t ask.)

Questions and Answers

Did you create this website?
Yes. I originally designed it in Framer, then rebuilt it using Jamstack and changed the design considerably.
What tools did you use?
Ghost as a headless CMS and Astro to render the site with a lot of custom enhancements. I also used Framer for the initial design and prototyping, Figma for some of the graphics, and Aseprite for pixelart.
What are these weird colors and fonts?
A nod to late-’80s CGA graphics.
What video game system do you recommend buying?
That would be the Nintendo Switch 2, thank you for asking.
